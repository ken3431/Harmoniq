Privacy Policy for Harmoniq

Last updated: December 2025

Harmoniq respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Harmoniq mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Harmoniq does not collect or store any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

The app may handle limited non-personal data, such as app usage and ad interactions, solely to ensure proper functionality.

2. Audio & App Usage

Harmoniq plays audio frequencies and sounds selected by the user. All audio playback occurs locally on your device.

  • No audio is recorded
  • No microphone access is used
  • No audio data is transmitted or stored externally

3. Advertisements

Harmoniq uses Google AdMob to display optional rewarded advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect certain information such as device data, ad interaction data, and anonymous usage statistics in accordance with their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Harmoniq does not access or store personal ad data.

4. Offline Use

Harmoniq can function offline once audio playback has started. An internet connection is not required for local sound playback.

5. Local Data Storage

Harmoniq may store limited data locally on your device, such as:

  • Timer settings
  • Daily ad limits

This data remains on your device and is not shared externally.

6. Children’s Privacy

Harmoniq does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe such information has been provided, please contact us.

7. Third-Party Services

Harmoniq uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
